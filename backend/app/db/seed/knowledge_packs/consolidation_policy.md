# NikkoGroup Consolidation Policy — FY2026

## Intercompany Elimination Rules

All intercompany transactions must be fully eliminated during the consolidation close process. IC balances are matched by invoice number, then by amount within a tolerance band of 2% of the transaction value, and finally by entity pair and period.

**IC differences greater than 2% of the transaction value require formal reconciliation before close sign-off.** The responsible controller in each entity must confirm the outstanding balance and provide a documented explanation for any variance exceeding this threshold. Unreconciled IC items above JPY 5M must be escalated to the Group Controller.

## Manual Adjustment Review Threshold

Any manual consolidation adjustment exceeding JPY 10M requires supporting documentation including: (1) the nature and business purpose of the adjustment, (2) reference to the underlying accounting standard or policy, and (3) approval by the entity controller and Group Finance. Goodwill-related adjustments must reference the most recent impairment test workpaper.

## Materiality

Group materiality for the consolidated financial statements is set at JPY 50M for the income statement and JPY 100M for the balance sheet. Items below materiality are still subject to review if they represent a trend, affect intercompany balances, or relate to key audit matters.
