# Quarterly Board Review — Agenda Template

## Standard Agenda Structure

1. **Financial Performance Summary** (15 min)
   - Group P&L: revenue, gross margin, operating income vs budget and prior year
   - Variance analysis by entity and key account
   - Cash flow and working capital overview

2. **Entity Performance Deep-Dive** (20 min)
   - Each entity: revenue drivers, margin trends, key wins/losses
   - Headcount and utilization metrics
   - Entity-specific risks and mitigations

3. **KPI Dashboard Review** (10 min)
   - SaaS metrics: MRR, churn, expansion revenue
   - Operational KPIs: utilization, DSO, NPS
   - Comparison to targets and industry benchmarks

4. **Risk and Escalation Items** (15 min)
   - Material variances exceeding 10% of budget
   - Client losses above USD 200K MRR impact
   - Regulatory or compliance issues
   - Intercompany reconciliation status

5. **Strategic Discussion Items** (20 min)
   - Pricing strategy vs growth mandate
   - Market entry decisions and investment cases
   - Acquisition integration updates

6. **Actions and Decisions Required** (10 min)
   - Summary of open items with owners and deadlines
   - Approval items for the committee
