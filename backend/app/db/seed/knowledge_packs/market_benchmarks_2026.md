# Technology Services Industry Benchmarks — 2026

## SaaS Metrics

- **Logo churn rate**: Median 2-3% monthly for mid-market; below 1% considered best-in-class. Enterprise segment typically 0.5-1.5%.
- **Net revenue retention (NRR)**: Top quartile above 120%; median 105-110%.
- **Gross margin**: Pure SaaS companies 70-80%; technology services blended (SaaS + consulting) 50-60%. Below 45% signals pricing or delivery cost issues.
- **CAC payback**: Median 12-18 months; above 24 months is a red flag.

## Operational Metrics

- **Days Sales Outstanding (DSO)**: Technology services median 45-55 days. Above 60 days indicates collection issues. Best practice is under 40 days.
- **Consulting utilization**: Target 75-80% for healthy margins. Below 70% signals overstaffing or demand weakness. Above 85% may indicate unsustainable pace.
- **Net Promoter Score (NPS)**: B2B technology median 40-50. Above 65 is considered excellent. Below 30 requires immediate attention.

## Financial Benchmarks

- **Operating margin**: Technology services 15-25%; pure SaaS 20-35% at scale.
- **Revenue per employee**: USD 200-350K for blended model; USD 300-500K for pure SaaS.
- **R&D spend as % of revenue**: 15-25% for growth-stage; 10-15% for mature.

## APAC Market Context

Technology services in APAC (especially Singapore) often show lower initial margins due to market entry pricing and higher contractor dependency. Margin improvement of 2-4 percentage points per year is typical as the entity scales and shifts to permanent staff.
